About The Shop:

Welcome to Sip N' Relax Coffee Shop, where the coffee is rich and the vibes are cozy. Our little haven was founded in 2011, with the vision of creating a warm nook in the heart of Dublin for coffee lovers. Whether you're seeking refuge from the daily hustle, catching up with friends, or fueling your day with our exquisite brews, Sip N' Relax is your go-to sanctuary.

Here, every sip is a testament to the art of coffee making. Our carefully curated menu boasts a diverse range of coffees and sumptuous pastries that are sure to tantalize your taste buds. Beyond the brews, our shop offers an inviting ambiance, with comfy seating and an interior that exudes rustic charm. It's not just a coffee shop - it's a home away from home.

Meet The Team:

The Coffee Maestro

Meet Michal, the heartbeat of Sip N' Relax. With a passion for coffee that knows no bounds, Michal is our head barista. He has honed the craft of brewing the perfect cup, blending art and science. His signature drinks have both soul and character, much like himself.

The Pastry Wizard

Next up is Ann, the genius behind our delectable pastries. With a twinkle in his/her eye and flour on his/her apron, Ann creates pastries that are not just treats but experiences. Her dedication to freshness and flavor makes every bite a moment to savor.

The Heart and Soul

Say hello to Sarah, who is arguably the first smile you'll see as you step into Sip N' Relax. As our front-of-house expert, Sarah ensures that every customer feels like they're stepping into a friend's living room. Her cheerfulness and knack for remembering regulars make her an indispensable part of our family.
