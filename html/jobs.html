Barista

Job Description:

We're seeking a passionate Barista to join our team. In this role, you'll craft various coffee drinks, maintain a clean workspace, and strive to provide excellent customer service. This role is ideal for coffee lovers with a knack for customer satisfaction.

Responsibilities:

  • Prepare and serve coffee and other drinks following standard recipes.
  • Maintain a clean and organized work area.
  • Provide excellent customer service.
  • Restock and manage inventory.
  • Handle cash and card transactions.
  • Work well within a team environment.

Pastry Chef

Job Description:

We're searching for a creative Pastry Chef to join our coffee shop. Your primary role will involve preparing delicious pastries and baked goods. This role is a perfect fit for individuals who enjoy working with fresh ingredients to create tempting treats.

Responsibilities:

  • Prepare and bake a wide range of pastries, cakes, and cookies.
  • Develop new recipes for seasonal items.
  • Ensure that quality and safety standards are met.
  • Maintain a clean and orderly kitchen.
  • Order and manage inventory of baking ingredients and supplies.

Job Description:

We're looking for a responsible Shift Supervisor to join our team. Your duties will include overseeing operations during assigned shifts and ensuring customer satisfaction. This role is crucial for maintaining a smooth and efficient coffee shop experience.

  • Supervise and guide staff during shifts.
