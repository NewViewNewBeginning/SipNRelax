Job Opportunity

Barista

Job Description:

Sip N' Relax Coffee Shop is seeking an enthusiastic and experienced Barista to join our team. The ideal candidate will have a passion for coffee and exceptional customer service skills. You will be responsible for crafting various coffee drinks, maintaining cleanliness, and ensuring customer satisfaction.

Responsibilities:

  • Prepare and serve coffee and other drinks following standard recipes.
  • Maintain a clean and organized work area.
  • Provide excellent customer service.
  • Restock and manage inventory.
  • Handle cash and card transactions.
  • Work well within a team environment.

Pastry Chef

Job Description:

We are looking for a talented Pastry Chef to prepare a variety of pastries and baked goods for our coffee shop. The ideal candidate should be skilled at creating high-quality pastries and have a keen eye for detail.

Responsibilities:

  • Prepare and bake a wide range of pastries, cakes, and cookies.
  • Develop new recipes for seasonal items.
  • Ensure that quality and safety standards are met.
  • Maintain a clean and orderly kitchen.
  • Order and manage inventory of baking ingredients and supplies.

Shift Supervisor

Job Description:

Sip N' Relax Coffee Shop is looking for a Shift Supervisor to oversee operations during assigned shifts. This role is crucial in ensuring customer satisfaction and smooth operations. The Shift Supervisor will lead the team in providing an exceptional coffee experience for our customers.

Responsibilities:

  • Supervise and guide staff during shifts.
  • Ensure outstanding customer service.
  • Handle customer complaints and feedback.
  • Monitor inventory levels and place orders as needed.
  • Maintain cleanliness and organization throughout the coffee shop.
  • Ensure compliance with health and safety regulations.
